About

Sri Ram Shalaka is a divine utility mentioned in the holy Book Shree Ram Charit Manas, capable of guiding humankind to right direction when in doubt, it hides nine verses or chopai of Philosophical meaning taken from Shree Ram Charit Manas, from which solution to any actions/questions related to our activities and lives are inferred and ingrained, in a square matrix of size 15x15. Each verse in shalaka has been represented by 25 unique akshars.
